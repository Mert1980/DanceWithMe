Our Goal

We created an alternative platform for dance lovers who would like to have partners to enjoy the amazing dance events with. There are applications such as "meetup.com" or "eventbrite.com" where you can see hundreds of events. If you do not have a partner, it makes no sense to join those dance events. Also, there are various matching platforms such as sportpartner.com where you can search for partners. But it makes no sense to find a dance partner if there is no dance event you can go with. Besides, it is time-consuming to search for ideal partners on those websites.

How does DanceWitnMe differentiate from other applications? We present dance events on your profile page, while our powerful matching engine finds the best partner for you! From now on you don't need to waste your precious time searching for dance events and partners!
