Our Goal

We want to create an alternative page for people who wish for dancing but who can not find out partners. We want to give people a resource with whom they can dance. We always try to get a dance partner through the internet, but soon we notice THERE IS A LOT OF INFORMATION about dance partners. Moreover, this ubiquitous and complicated information consumes energy and takes a lot of time.

Finding a dance partner is like looking a friend, however, it is harder since you have to not only find someone you are compatible with but also can dance. A lot of people have this problem, and our team wants to solve it. Therefore, our team created dancewithme.com and organized all the steps for you to meet your dream dance partner easily.
